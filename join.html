JOIN DAOMA

It has always been our goal to provide the lectures without ticketed admissions as a public service that advocates for the value of architecture and the design arts. We hope that you will embrace this change and continue to support our mission, because without your support we would be unable to make such a bold move. We recognize the risk we assume but this is a goal we have been working to achieve since daOMA was established in 2006. We hope that we can continue to count on the support of you our members and patrons to make this happen this season.
